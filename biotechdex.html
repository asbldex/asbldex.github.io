BIOTECHDEX


App encyclopedia

Mobile, iPad and web application (in development) to explore biology and biotechnology natively on mobile

Functionality:

1. Explore genes (NCBI gene)
2. Explore KEGG, BRENDA and other databases
3. Explore UniProt
4. Explore GEO (gene expression omnibus)
5. Upload RNAseq file for cell types by GEO match
6. Execute BLAST
7. Systems biology sim with whole-cell model. Tweak medium glucose, add differentiation factors, throw in challengers (antibodies, competing species) to grow simulated cellular life.
8. Connect your lab notebook for in silico experiments and data analysis from machine readouts
9. Share your biotechdex with other bioengineers

Biotechdex is the starting point of mobile biotechnology. One day, ASBL machines will miniaturize to the point of sequencing and biomarker identification on phones. Spit on phone to decode saliva biology, powered by Biotechdex and ASBL under the hood.
