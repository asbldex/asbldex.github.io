Automated Synthetic Biology Labs

ASBL makes machines for keyboard biotechnology. Ditch the gloves, be a keyboard bioengineer.

ASBL is a biotechnology company developing drugs and machines for bioengineers. We develop drugs for nonalcoholic fatty liver disease and type 2 diabetes. Our machines are ASBL Lab 0, ASBL 1, and ASBL II. Biotechnology should be accessible to everyone. An ASBL machine is an Automated Synthetic Biology Lab. ASBL simplifies biotechnology so anyone can do it without an expensive lab or PhD training. ASBL machine protocol is our language of biotechnology. Think of it as Assembly language for biotechnology. ASBL machine protocol is the language on which ASBL machines operate.

Our drug development effort uses cutting edge biotechnology including Deconstructed Human organoid chips, high-throughput assays, and targeted delivery. Our drugs bioengineer by harnessing the human body's innate mechanisms to target inflammation and glucose imbalance.

We build and freely release computational biology software:
1. HumanGEMlib Python library
2. Bioreactor Model Python library
3. Protocol Dex biotechnology protocol index

ASBL Inc. is incorporated in Delaware, United States and headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, United States.
